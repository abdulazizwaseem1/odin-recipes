Simple Custard

Description

Simple Custard is a smooth, creamy dessert that highlights the comforting flavors of milk, eggs, sugar, and vanilla. This recipe gently cooks milk with sugar until warm, then slowly incorporates beaten eggs to create a silky mixture without curdling. Vanilla extract is added for aroma and flavor, and the custard is cooked over low heat until thickened to a luscious consistency. The result is a versatile treat that can be enjoyed on its own, poured over fruit, or used as a filling for pastries. Its delicate sweetness and velvety texture make it a timeless classic, perfect for both everyday indulgence and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place milk, vanilla extract, and butter in a saucepan.
  3. Cook and stir over medium heat until simmering; remove from heat before it comes to a boil.
  4. Whisk eggs, sugar, and cornstarch together in a bowl until sugar dissolves.
  5. Set the saucepan back over low heat. Pour in egg mixture slowly, whisking constantly, until custard thickens enough to coat the bottom of a spoon, 5 to 10 minutes.
  6. Serve warm or transfer custard into a large bowl and cover.
  7. Allow to cool before chilling. Enjoy!
