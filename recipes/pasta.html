Alfredo Pasta

Description

Mushroom Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is a rich, creamy, and indulgent dish that combines tender fettuccine with a velvety Alfredo sauce, savory mushrooms, and succulent shrimp. The recipe begins with cooking pasta until al dente, then tossing it in a sauce made from butter, heavy cream, Romano, and Parmesan cheeses, infused with garlic for depth of flavor. Meanwhile, onions and mushrooms are sautéed until soft and aromatic, before shrimp is added and cooked to a perfect pink. The shrimp and mushroom mixture is folded into the Alfredo sauce, coating the pasta in a luxurious blend of creaminess and umami. Finished with a sprinkle of parsley, this dish balances the richness of the sauce with the earthiness of mushrooms and the delicate sweetness of shrimp, making it a comforting yet elegant meal ideal for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) box dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 ¼ cups butter, divided
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed, divided
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces diced mushrooms
  • 1 (8 ounce) package frozen shrimp, deveined and tails removed, thawed
